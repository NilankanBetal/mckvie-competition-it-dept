About TRACE.IT

Trace.IT 2K22 is the first iteration of an yearly technical event organized by the students and faculty of the IT Department of MCKV Institute of Engineering.

Students of all departments will compete in teams to prove their knowledge and skills.

The event comprises of 3 rounds as follows:

Round 1

Triviac

MCQ-based questions based on aptitude, Coding, and GK

Round 2

Crack-a-code

Cracking hints and riddles will give access to the question paper that participants will need to solve on machines

Round 3

Blind Coding

Coding questions needed to be solved by participants which will be judged on the basis of speed( the screen will be hidden)

Prizes

What's in it for me..?


5 MAR Points guaranteed on registration and participation.
E-Certificate for all participants and physical certificates for top 3 teams.
Refreshments for all participants who qualify for Round 2.
Exiciting prizes for Top 3 teams + physical certificates.
